Time Based Furnaces
-------------------

Version: 70160
Author: fairiestoy

Description:
------------

This furnaces have the goal to work without ABMs or
NodeTimers and should be resistend against lags, since
they are calculating the results based on the time
passed by since last right-clicking it.
